Repairing a Tails USB stick that fails to start after an upgrade
← Upgrading a Tails USB stick | Documentation | Resetting a USB stick using Linux →

Sometimes, after an automatic upgrade, your Tails USB stick might either:

  • Fail to start, often after the message:

    Loading, please wait...

  • Behave weirdly. For example, your keyboard does not work or you cannot connect to a network.

To fix this problem, please try to do a manual upgrade.

The Persistent Storage on the USB stick will be preserved.
