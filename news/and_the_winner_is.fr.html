Et le gagnant est... 2014-04-12

Our logo contest ended up a few days ago. Since then, 11 regular Tails contributors voted on the 36 proposals.

Gagnant

The winning proposal is the one by Tchou.

We commented on the initial version and we already came up with an improved version: logo/tchou-improved.png

Félicitations !

Pendant les prochains jours, nous allons terminer les dernières finitions sur le logo et l'intégrer à Tails 1.0. N'hésitez pas à le commenter.

Top 7
