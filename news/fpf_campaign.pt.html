Crowd-funding campaign for Tails 2013-12-15

The Freedom of the Press Foundation launched a campaign to support encryption tools for journalists that is going to last for two months. The campaign is gathering funds for Tails, Tor, the encrypted mobile communication tools RedPhone and TextSecure, and the encrypted email platform LEAP.

https://pressfreedomfoundation.org/

Spread the word and help make the campaign a success. This is an easy way to donate to support open-source encryption tools that can better protect the communications of journalists and sources. Your donation will be tax-deductible. You can also donate by check.
