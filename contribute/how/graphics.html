Design graphics for Tails

You can improve how Tails looks, and make it easier to promote.

  1. Known tasks
  2. Other ideas
  3. Talk to us

Known tasks

The issues with the T:Graphics label in GitLab correspond to things that would benefit from the work of graphic designers.

Other ideas

If you are a graphic designer, you can surely find other ideas to improve Tails! Even if you don't implement your suggestions yourself, create issues with your ideas so that others can benefit from your insight.

Talk to us

You can subscribe to tails-dev@boum.org, our development mailing list.
